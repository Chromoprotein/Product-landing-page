How it's made

We start making a Plumbus by rolling out the dinglebop and then adding the schleem. You'll notice there isn't any schleem in our plumbuses though, we remove it all before going to the next step! Don't worry, we re-purpose the schleem for other uses.

After we stretch the Dinglebop over and into the grumbo to get the right shape, we need to rub it with some fleeb juice. To do this, we grab the fleeb and rub it all over the tip of the Dinglebop.

Our Plumbus is almost ready but its still trapped in the Grumbo. We chisel the Grumbo away so we can send it on to it's new home.

And that is how a Plumbus is made! Would you like to purchase one?
